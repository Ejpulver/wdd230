Target Audience

The target audience is people in business in the local area who want to get together and support one another. I would imagine these people either would want to give support or get support from other businesses, depending on their experiences or size of the business.

Personas

Ryan

  • Is a new business owner and knows he needs the support of the chamber of commerce to become integrated into the community.
  • Wants to support others as well
  • Age 30

Alyssa

  • Has a successful business in the area, and wants to take it to the next level.
  • Doesn't feel like she is in the know about what is going on in the city of Provo.
  • Age 37

Scenarios

  • How do I join the Chamber of Commerce?
  • What are the benefits of joining the chamber of commerce
  • How can I promote myself to the members of the chamber?
  • What resources are out there for me?
  • How can I contribute?
